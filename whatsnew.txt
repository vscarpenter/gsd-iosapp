iOS — recommended

▎ This update is a focused polish pass: better contrast, calmer dashboard, and safer editing.
▎
▎ • Undo for deleted tasks. Delete a task by accident? An Undo option appears right after — restore it exactly as it was, online or off.
▎ • Easier to read. Sharper contrast on due dates, counts, and chart labels throughout the app, plus larger tap targets on card menus and tag removal.
▎ • iPad editor no longer clips. The task editor now opens at full size on iPad; on iPhone, a "Show all details" button reveals every section.
▎ • Calmer dashboard. The overdue reminder is now a normal card instead of a red alert banner, and the streak tiles are gone for good.
▎ • More private by default. Fetching a page title when you share a link is now off unless you turn it on — and only ever touches links you explicitly share.
▎ • A round of smaller polish: consistent sign-in buttons, one toggle color throughout Settings, and clearer onboarding copy.
▎
▎ As always, GSD works fully offline — syncing is optional and only happens when you're signed in.
